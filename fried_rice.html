Fried Rice

DISCRIPTIONS:-

  • Here is a delicious fried rice made specially from chichen republic kitchen, It's made with love and joy best for your happy weekend with family.

INGRIDIENTS LIST

  • 4 cups long-grain rice (parboil)
  • 500g-1kg chicken (or turkey), cut into pieces + 200-300g chicken liver
  • Mixed vegetables: 2 cups diced carrots, 2 cups green peas, 1 cup sweet corn, 1 cup green peas, 1 cup slice cabbage/bell peppers
  • 1-2 onions (chopped)
  • 1/2-3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2-3 tbsp curry powder, 1 tsp thyme, white/black pepper
  • 3-4 seasoning cubes, salt to taste
  • Chicken stock (fromm boiling meats) for cooking rice
  • Optional: prawns, garlic/ginger paste

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Seasoning and boilchicken pieces with onions, seasoning, thyme, and curry until tender. Reserve the stock, cut some chicken into small chunks.
  2. Parboil rice in plian water for 5-10 minutes, drain, and set aside
  3. In a larg wok or pot, heat oil. Fry chopped onions, garlic/ginger, and chicken liver/chunks until browned. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same oil, stir-fry the hard vegetables (carrots, green beans) for 3-5 munites, then add softer ones (peas, corn, cabbage). Seasoning with curry , thyme, pepper, and cubes.
  5. Add the parboiled rice and stir-fry to coat evenly with oil and veggies.
  6. Pour in chicken stock (enough to finish cooking the rice, about 1:1 or slightly less). Cover and cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes until rice is tender and flavors absorbed.
  7. Return the fried chicken/liver to the pot, gently mix, and steam for another 5 minutes. Sserve hot, often paired with peppered chicken or a light soup/sauce on the side.
