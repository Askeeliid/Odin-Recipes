Delicious Nigeria Jollof

DISCRIPIONS:-

  • Here is a delicious jollof rice made specially by a nigerian mother, It's made with love and joy best for your happy weekend with family.

INGRIDIENTS LIST

  • 4-5 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 6-8 ripe plum tomatoes + 3-4 red bell peppers + 1-2 scotch bonnet peppers +1-2 onions (blended for sauce)
  • 4-6tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2-3/4 cup vegetable or palm oil
  • 1-2 medium onions (sliced)
  • 3-4 chicken/beef stock (or water + extra seasoning)
  • 2-3 bay leaves , 1 tbsp thyme, 1-2 tbsp curry powder
  • 3-4seasoning cubes, salt to taste
  • Optional, garlic, ginger, white pepper

STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers and onions into smooth puree. set aside.
  2. Rinse rice thoroughly until water runs clear (optional: parboil for 5-10 minutes and drainto reduce starch).
  3. In a larg pot, heat oil. Fry sliced onions until translucent, then add paste and fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in the blended pepper puree, add seasoning (thyme, curry, bay leaves, cubes), and cook for medium heat for 15-20 minutes until thick and oil seperats (this builds the flavor base).
  5. Add the stock or water (should about 1.5:1 liquid to rice ratio). Bring to a boil, taste and adjust salt/seasoning.
  6. Add the rice, stir once to combine , reduce heat to blow, cover tightli, and cook for 20-30 minutes. Avoid peeking too often.
  7. Once done, fluff with a fork.
