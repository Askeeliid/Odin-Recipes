Egusi Soup

DISCRIPTIONS:-

  • Here is a very delicious african egusi soup, it goes so well with any type of swallow. Serve hot with fufu, pounded yam, or rice.

INGRIDIENTS LIST

  • 2-3 cups ground egusi (melon)
  • 1-1.5 assorted meats (beef, goat meat, trip/shaki, ponmo cow leg)
  • Stockfish and smoked fish
  • 1 cup of palm oil
  • 4 red bell peppers (tatashe) + 2-24 scotch bonnet peppers (atarodo) + 1-2 onions (blended)
  • 1-1 chopped ugu leaves (or spinach/shoko)
  • 3-4 tbsp ground crayfish
  • 2-3 seasoning cubes (maggi/knor)
  • Salt to taste

STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS"

  1. Wash and season the assorted meats with salt, onions, seasoning cubes, and some water. Boil untill ender (45-60 minuts), reserving the stock.
  2. Biend the peppers, onions, and some tomatoes if desiers untill smooth.
  3. In a larg pot, heat the palm oil untill hot.Add the blended pepper mix and for 10-15 minutes untill the raw taste is gone and oil floats on top.
  4. Add the crayfish, seasoning cubes, and some stock, stir well.
  5. Mix the groung egusi with the little water or stock a thick paste. Add small lumps of cook for 10-15 minutes without stirinf too much (this helps it form nice chunks).
  6. Add the coocked meats, stockfish, and smoked fish and stir gently and simmer for anothe 5-10 minutes.
  7. Add the chopped ugu leaves , taste and adjust seasoning. Simmer for 3-5 more minutes untill leaves are wilted but still green. serve hot with fufu, pounded yam, or rice.
